Punishment.

For Family Imposition the Punishment of an Imposer
himself shall be as follows.

1. He shall be punished ignominiously, and afterly, with the
Imposers Mark, viz [I]mposition.

2. He shall be imprisoned or confined to hard Labour for Life.

The Punishment of a Suborner (A) shall be as follows.

1. He shall be ignominiously connected with an Imposer in the affix
of an Imposer.

2. He shall be imprisoned or confined to hard Labour for Life.

Exposition.

[A] Suborner. There may be a suborner of imposition, when there is no
imposer: the party falsely pretended to be the father in question
not knowing that he is intended to be so, for instance in the case of a suppo-
sititious Child.
