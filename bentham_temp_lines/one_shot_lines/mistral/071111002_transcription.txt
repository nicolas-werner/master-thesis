Punishment.
For Family Imposture the Punishment of an Impostor
himself shall be as follows:
He shall be punished ignominiously and afflictively with the
Impostor's Mask. See [Punishments]
She shall be imprisoned or confined to hard Labour for Life.
The Punishment of a Suborner shall be as follows:
He shall be ignominiously connected with an Impostor or the Effigies
94 of an Impostor.
He shall be imprisoned or confined to hard Labour for Life.
