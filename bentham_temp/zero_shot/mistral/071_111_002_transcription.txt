Punishment.

For Family Imposition the Punishment of an Imposer
himself shall be as follows.

1. He shall be punished ignominiously, and afterly, with the
Imposers Mark, viz [I]mposition.

2. He shall be imprisoned or confined to hard Labour for Life.

The Punishment of a Suborner shall be as follows.

1. He shall be ignominiously connected with an Imposer in the off-
ence of an Imposer.

2. He shall be imprisoned or confined to hard Labour for Life.

Exposition.

[Suborner] There may be a suborner of imposition, when there is no
imposer: the party falsely pretended to be subject to the character in question
not knowing that he is entitled to it, for instance in the case of a suppo-
sititious child.
